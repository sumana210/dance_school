Welcome to ABC Dance Academy

ABC Dance Academy is one of the best dance schools in town, with an array of dance classes and sessions for the students. It is one of the very few institutes in the heart of the city to offer both Western Classical and Indian genres on Ballet, Waltz, Ballroom, Contemporary, Hip hop, Jazz, Tap dance, Folk dance, Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Kathakali, Sattriya, Manipuri and Mohiniyattam.

Committed to spreading and expansion of the dance community, the ABC Dance Academy holds professional training and grooming through the dance classes. A speculative learning opportunity is offered to the students.

ABC Dance Academy is an institution that personifies excellence in every form of Dance. The Institute is dedicated towards supplementing creativity, knowledge & performance in dance & preparing students for a variety of professions in these fields.
